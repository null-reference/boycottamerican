Global Movement to Boycott American Products

In response to the trade war initiated by the United States against Canada, we're building a global coalition to support alternatives to American products and services.

Economic Impact

The trade war initiated by the United States has significant economic consequences for bilateral relations, exports, and jobs. Here's a breakdown of the current situation:

The global community stands in solidarity with Canada and other countries affected by these unilateral trade actions. By making conscious consumer choices, we can shift market demand and send a clear message.

Affected Industries

The trade war has impacted numerous industries across the global market. Here are some of the key sectors experiencing the most significant effects:

Agriculture & Food

Dairy products, grains, produce, and processed foods face substantial tariffs and trade barriers.

Technology

Hardware, software, and digital services experiencing disruption in global supply chains.

Automotive

Vehicle manufacturing, parts, and accessories facing increased costs and regulatory hurdles.

Energy

Oil, natural gas, and renewable energy sectors being reshaped by changing trade patterns.

Forestry & Lumber

Wood products and paper goods suffering from unpredictable pricing and availability.

Manufacturing

Consumer goods, machinery, and industrial equipment encountering significant export barriers.
